Features

  • - Puzzle Meets Design: Combine tile-matching mechanics with creative logo-building.
  • - Unlock Brand Assets: Generate fonts, icons, and palettes to assemble unique identities.
  • - Themed Levels: Journey through stages inspired by startups, local businesses, and international brands.
  • - Merge Mechanics: Fuse assets to create custom logos that grow with your progress.
  • - Earn and Upgrade: Solve puzzles to earn currency and unlock premium design tools for more options.
  • - Compete and Share: Climb weekly leaderboards in branding contests and showcase your creations in a virtual showroom.
