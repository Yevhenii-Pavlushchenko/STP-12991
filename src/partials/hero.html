Welcome and Introduction

Step into the world of BrandMaster, where creativity meets strategy in a casual yet captivating tile-matching puzzle experience. Here, every match you make unlocks pieces of a brand’s identity — fonts, icons, and color palettes — that you can merge into one-of-a-kind logos.

Whether you’re a casual gamer or a budding designer, BrandMaster invites you to solve colorful, shape-based puzzles and build unique brands that tell a story. From launching startups to crafting global icons, every level challenges your wit and design sense.
