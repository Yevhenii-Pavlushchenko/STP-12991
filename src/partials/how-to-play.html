How to Play

BrandMaster’s gameplay is simple to pick up but rewarding to master. Match tiles by color and shape to generate essential brand elements — fonts, icons, and color swatches. As you progress, merge these elements to craft custom logos that reflect your creative vision.

Each level introduces new puzzles and design assets inspired by real-world branding challenges. Some levels require sharp logic to unlock premium resources, while others test your ability to merge assets efficiently.

No prior design experience needed—just your imagination and a knack for puzzles.
